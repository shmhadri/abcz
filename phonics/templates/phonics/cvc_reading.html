I أنا - Examples

😊 I am happy.
أنا سعيد.
📚 I love reading books.
أحب قراءة الكتب.
⚽ I play football every day.
ألعب كرة القدم كل يوم.
🎨 I can draw very well.
أستطيع الرسم بشكل جيد.
🍕 I like pizza and ice cream.
أحب البيتزا والآيس كريم.
🏫 I go to school by bus.
أذهب إلى المدرسة بالباص.
👨‍👩‍👧 I have a big family.
لدي عائلة كبيرة.
🎵 I listen to music daily.
أستمع للموسيقى يومياً.
🌟 I want to be a doctor.
أريد أن أكون طبيباً.
🐕 I have a cute dog.
لدي كلب لطيف.

🎯 Quiz Time - اختبر نفسك!

15 أسئلة على الضمائر والملكيات - Choose the correct answer!

❶ Choose the correct pronoun:
___ am a student.
❷ Fill in the blank:
This book belongs to ___.
❸ Choose the possessive adjective:
This is ___ pen. (It belongs to me)
❹ Select the correct pronoun:
___ is my best friend. (talking about Ali)
❺ Which is the possessive pronoun?
The red car is ___. (It belongs to me)
❻ Fill in with object pronoun:
Sara loves ___. (talking about Ahmad)
❼ Choose correctly:
___ are playing football. (We're talking about Fatima and Layla)
❽ Possessive form:
Those books are ___. (They belong to you)
❾ Find the right pronoun:
___ lives in a big house. (talking about Mona)
❿ Object pronoun needed:
Can you help ___? (talking about Noor and me)
⓫ Possessive adjective:
___ house is very beautiful. (talking about Sara)
⓬ Which one is correct?
___ is very cute. (talking about a cat)
⓭ Possessive pronoun:
This pencil is ___. (It belongs to him)
⓮ Complete the sentence:
___ are learning English. (talking about my classmates and me)
⓯ Final question - Possessive:
The toys are ___. (They belong to them)
